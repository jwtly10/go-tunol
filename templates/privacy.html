Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • GitHub username (through GitHub OAuth authentication)
  • Basic account management information
  • Service usage metrics (such as connection times and API token creation)

2. Data Privacy in Tunneling

We do not monitor, store, or log any data that passes through our tunneling service. All proxied data remains private and is not tracked or stored by go-tunol.

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Service provision and maintenance
  • Security and fraud prevention

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The only exception is GitHub, which we use for authentication purposes.

5. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
